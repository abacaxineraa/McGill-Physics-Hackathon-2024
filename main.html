Relatively Monstrous

  • Survive the swarm! Move Slimey with the arrow keys or WASD keys to avoid the endless monsters! If Slimey bumps into a monster, it's game over!
  • Aim with precision! Monsters move at near light speed - predict their paths and shoot the photon beam wisely with left-click!
  • Adapt to physics! Use relativistic effects to your advantage!
  • Be careful! You can only shoot monsters when they are glowing! High energy photons emitted by some fallen monsters will twist time and space - stay on your slime toes or you might get slimed by the effects!
